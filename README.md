🌟 Exploring Hybrid Electric Systems with AI-Driven Models 🌟 

We are thrilled to share our ongoing research into hybrid electric systems! Together with my partner Anuj Srinivas, we developed an AI-driven model using **Reinforcement Learning (PPO)** to optimize energy efficiency, noise reduction, and turbine-motor coordination in hybrid electric vehicles. 

Our efforts have yielded "impressive results", with a "final episode score of 123", showcasing the model's ability to adapt and perform effectively. The consistently "increasing performance graph" highlights the potential of our approach for real-world applications. Additionally, we explored traditional ML techniques like "Linear Regression" and "Random Forest", further validating our outcomes with strong metrics. 

📊 Key Achievements: 
- Successfully trained the PPO agent for noise reduction and energy efficiency optimization. 
- Predicted next-state transitions with promising accuracy and reliability. 
- Achieved episode scores that demonstrate significant progress, including the final score of 123.62 
- Evaluated model accuracy with metrics like R-squared, MSE, and Explained Variance.
